What is included in price?

EMS Suit

The suit integrates all electrodes and sensors into the fabric. Patented dry electrodes in the Suit make moisture on the skin a conductor. Thanks to that, You are able to carry out EMS training without additional wetting and without extra clothing. Moreover, It remains flexible, durable and easy to care for.

EMS Connector

The connector is the heart of the entire system. It's a device that connects the suit to your mobile. This little device controls the power and flow of electrical impulses that make EMS training so effective. It connects via Bluetooth with iOS and Android systems.

App for Mobile/Tablet

The intuitive user interface on the iOS and Android app makes it easy to control your EMS training with EasyMotionSkin. From the moment of logging in to the end of the training, the application focuses on the most important elements and its setting is child's play.
