Benefits of training in an EMS suit

35% Higher endurance

EMS training uses electrical impulses to stimulate the muscles, which leads to intense contraction and work of the muscles. Thanks to this, it is possible to achieve greater muscle activation compared to traditional exercises.

33% Higher strength

In a conventional workout, only 30% of the muscles are being activated each time, however, with EasyMotionSkin, between 70%-80% of the muscle groups are activated simultaneously.

3 x more calories burned

EMS training can help reduce body fat through increased calorie burning and metabolic activation. The intensity of training allows for effective use of energy and stimulation of fat burning processes.
