EMS Mobile App

Intuitive App's Interface

The intuitive user interface on the iOS and Android app makes it easy to control your EMS training with EasyMotionSkin. From the moment of logging in to the end of the training, the application focuses on the most important elements and its setting is child's play.

Multiple Training Programs

There are over thirty different training programs available in the application. The different workout categories range from 'Strength', 'Metabolism/Cellulite', 'Advanced', 'Fat Burn' and 'Endurance' to 'Relaxation'. Individual frequencies and time sequences are assigned to each category. This provides a wide range of conveniently arranged training programs.

Electrodes Power Control

The main screen of the application is used for precise adjustment of individual groups of electrodes. You can choose all eight zones at once, individually or combine several of them together. Change between pause phase and stimulation is always visible. Thanks to the intelligently created and easy to understand the application EMS training becomes a unique experience. The updates will let you take advantage of the constantly improved app.
