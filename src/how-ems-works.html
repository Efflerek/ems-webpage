Stimulate Your Muscles

What is an electrostimulation training?

EMS training uses electrical impulses to stimulate the muscles, which allows for effective strengthening muscle and fat burning. During training, electrodes are placed on the body of the exercising person, which send electrical impulses to the muscles. In response to these impulses, the muscles will contract and relax, which is very similar to the natural movement of muscles during exercise. EMS training allows you to effective muscle strengthening in a short time, which is especially beneficial for people who want to reduce excess weight.
